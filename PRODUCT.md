# AttendU - Smart Attendance System

## Product Purpose
A smart attendance verification system for educational institutions using multi-factor authentication (RFID card, facial recognition, manual entry) to streamline classroom attendance tracking with real-time data collection and reporting.

## Users
- **Instructors**: Mark attendance during class sessions, view student records, export reports
- **Administrators**: Manage instructors, courses, sections, system settings, view institution-wide analytics
- **Students**: Check-in via RFID/face recognition or manual entry (passive participants)

## Brand Tone
- **Professional yet approachable**: Education tech, not cold corporate
- **Trustworthy & secure**: Privacy and data integrity matter (biometric data)
- **Efficient & clear**: Instructors need to start sessions and verify attendance quickly
- **Delightful**: Micro-interactions and animation to build confidence and reduce friction

## Strategic Principles
1. **Speed**: Minimize clicks to start attendance session or check-in students
2. **Clarity**: Obvious visual feedback for device status, attendance states, and errors
3. **Trust**: Clear indication of verification method (RFID vs. Face vs. Manual)
4. **Accessibility**: Works for instructors in various lighting (classroom, outdoor), various abilities
5. **Brand consistency**: Professional institution context; logo and colors reflect educational authority

## Anti-References
- Avoid: Generic SaaS template aesthetics, overly playful tone, dark/neon crypto vibes
- Avoid: Overly complex first-time setup; instructors should be productive in 2 minutes
- Avoid: Suspicious/surveillance feel; frame biometrics as convenience, not Big Brother

## Register
**Product** (design serves the tool, not the other way around)
